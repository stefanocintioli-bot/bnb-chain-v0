# BNB Chain — v0 Brand System

Paste this at the start of any v0 project to enforce BNB Chain brand identity.

## COLOR PALETTE
--bg-primary: #0D0D0D
--bg-card: rgba(255,255,255,0.05)
--bg-card-hover: rgba(255,255,255,0.08)
--gold: #F0B90B
--gold-muted: rgba(240,185,11,0.3)
--text-primary: #FFFFFF
--text-secondary: #888888
--border-subtle: rgba(255,255,255,0.08)
--border-gold: rgba(240,185,11,0.3)

Rule: dark backgrounds only. Never white or light backgrounds.
Rule: gold is for headlines, highlights, borders, CTAs only.
Rule: no purple, no gradients on backgrounds, no generic AI aesthetics.

## TYPOGRAPHY
Display/Headlines: Syne (700, 800) — Google Fonts
Body/Labels/Stats: DM Mono (400, 500) — Google Fonts
Never use: Inter, Roboto, Arial, system fonts

Sizes:
- Hero title: clamp(2.5rem, 6vw, 5rem) Syne 800
- Section title: clamp(1.4rem, 3vw, 2.2rem) Syne 700
- Body text: 0.95rem DM Mono 400, line-height 1.7
- Stat numbers: clamp(2rem, 4vw, 3.5rem) Syne 800
- Labels: 0.75rem DM Mono 400 color #888888
- Letter spacing on all-caps labels: 0.1em

## LOGO USAGE
Always inline SVG — never img src tags.
Top-left corner every slide: 24px from edges.
Width: 120px for nav/slides, 200px for covers.
On dark backgrounds: white logo version.
On gold backgrounds: black logo version.

Logo files in logos/ folder:
- BNB_Chain_Logo_White.svg — horizontal white (default)
- BNB_Chain_Logo_Black.svg — horizontal black (for yellow slides)
- BNB_Chain_Logo_Yellow.svg — horizontal yellow (special use)
- BNB_Chain_Stacked_Logo_White.svg — stacked white (for covers)
- BNB_Chain_Symbol_White.svg — symbol only white
- BNB_Chain_Symbol_Yellow.svg — symbol only gold

## PRESENTATION RULES
Max 4 sentences per slide — strictly enforced.
Many slides over few dense slides.
Logo top-left every slide.
Progress bar: 2px gold line at bottom, fills left to right.
Slide counter: "01 / 10" DM Mono #888888 bottom-right.
Navigation: arrow keys + touch swipe + circular (last to first).
Transitions: 350ms ease CSS only.
Background pattern: subtle dot grid radial-gradient opacity 0.04.

## CARD COMPONENT STANDARD
Background: rgba(255,255,255,0.05)
Border: 1px solid rgba(255,255,255,0.08)
Border-radius: 12px
Padding: 24px
Left accent on key cards: 3px solid #F0B90B
Hover: border-color rgba(240,185,11,0.4) + scale(1.02)
Transition: all 200ms ease

## CONTENT RULES
No hashtags on official BNB Chain content.
No financial advice, no price speculation.
Tech-first messaging always.
Argentine Spanish for LatAm community content.
English for international and BD content.
Tone: builder-first, direct, energetic — never corporate or stiff.

## HOW TO USE IN V0
1. Start a new v0 chat
2. Paste the full contents of this file as your first message
3. Then describe what you want to build
4. v0 applies BNB Chain brand automatically

For presentations also add:
"Follow Artu Grande presentation principles: max 4 sentences per slide,
many slides, logo top-left, progress bar, CTA with QR at end."
